Chart Interpretation Guide

Learn how to interpret charts and tables effectively for exams and presentations.

General Instructions

Follow these steps to interpret charts effectively:

  • Understand the Chart: Identify the type of chart and its purpose.
  • Analyze Key Components: Examine titles, labels, and data sources.
  • Identify Trends: Look for patterns, peaks, and comparisons.
  • Use Descriptive Language: Use terms like "rise," "fall," "peak," and "trend."
  • Structure Your Interpretation: Start with an introduction, describe key findings, and conclude with a summary.
