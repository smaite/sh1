Application Writing

Write effective job and leave applications

Application Format

What is an Application?

An application is a formal written request submitted to an authority for a specific purpose such as seeking a job, requesting leave, or asking for permission.

Types of Applications

  • Job Application: Applying for employment
  • Leave Application: Requesting time off
  • Permission Application: Asking for approval
  • Character Certificate: Requesting documents
  • Transfer Certificate: School/College transfers

Standard Format

  1. From: Sender's address (top left)
  2. To: Receiver's address/designation
  3. Date: Date of writing
  4. Subject: Brief purpose (one line)
  5. Salutation: Respected Sir/Madam, Dear Principal
  6. Body:
    • Paragraph 1: Purpose of writing
    • Paragraph 2: Details and reasons
    • Paragraph 3: Request and gratitude
  7. Closing: Thanking you / Yours obediently
  8. Signature: Name, Class/Roll (if student)

Job Application Essentials

  • Reference: Where you saw the vacancy
  • Qualifications: Educational background
  • Experience: Relevant work history
  • Skills: Special abilities relevant to job
  • Bio-data/CV: Attached documents

Leave Application Essentials

  • Duration: Number of days and dates
  • Reason: Genuine cause for leave
  • Arrangements: Who will cover your work
  • Contact: How to reach you if needed

Writing Tips

  • Be polite and respectful
  • Be specific about dates and details
  • Keep it concise (150-200 words)
  • Use formal language and proper format
  • Proofread before submitting
  • Mention enclosures if any

Practice Topics

Easy Level

Sick leave, Permission for event

Medium Level

Job application, Transfer request

Hard Level

Leave for special reasons, Scholarship
