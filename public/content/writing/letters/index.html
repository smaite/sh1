Letter Writing

Master formal and informal letter formats

Letter Format

Types of Letters

  • Formal Letters: To officials, teachers, organizations
  • Informal Letters: To friends, family, relatives
  • Semi-formal Letters: To acquaintances, neighbors

Formal Letter Format

  1. Sender's Address: Top right corner
  2. Date: Below sender's address
  3. Receiver's Address: Left side below date
  4. Subject: Brief topic of the letter
  5. Salutation: Dear Sir/Madam, Dear Mr./Ms. [Name]
  6. Body: Introduction, Main content, Conclusion
  7. Closing: Yours faithfully/sincerely/truly
  8. Signature: Name and designation

Informal Letter Format

  1. Sender's Address: Top right (optional)
  2. Date: Below address
  3. Salutation: Dear [First name], My dear [Name]
  4. Opening: Ask about well-being
  5. Body: Share news, experiences, feelings
  6. Closing: Best wishes, Take care, Love
  7. Signature: Your name/nickname

Language Differences

Aspect Formal Informal
Contractions Avoid (do not, cannot) Use (don't, can't)
Vocabulary Sophisticated, professional Simple, casual
Sentences Complex, complete Short, fragments OK

Writing Tips

  • Be clear about the purpose of your letter
  • Maintain consistent tone throughout
  • Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences)
  • Proofread for grammar and spelling
  • Use appropriate opening and closing phrases
