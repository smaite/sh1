Recipe Structure

What is a Recipe?

A recipe is a set of instructions for preparing a particular dish, including a list of ingredients and the steps to follow.

Components of a Recipe

  • Title: Name of the dish
  • Introduction: Brief description of the dish
  • Preparation Time: How long it takes to prepare
  • Cooking Time: How long it takes to cook
  • Servings: Number of people it serves
  • Ingredients: List with measurements
  • Method: Step-by-step instructions
  • Tips (optional): Serving suggestions or variations

Measurement Vocabulary

  • Weight: gram (g), kilogram (kg), pound (lb)
  • Volume: teaspoon (tsp), tablespoon (tbsp), cup, liter, milliliter (ml)
  • Quantity: piece, slice, bunch, pinch, handful

Cooking Verbs

  • Preparation: chop, dice, slice, grate, peel, wash, drain
  • Cooking Methods: boil, fry, bake, roast, steam, grill, simmer
  • Mixing: mix, stir, blend, whisk, fold, combine
  • Other: heat, cool, serve, garnish, decorate

Time Expressions

  • First, Next, Then, After that, Finally
  • For 5 minutes, Until golden brown
  • While stirring continuously, Before serving

Writing Tips

  • List ingredients in order of use
  • Be specific with measurements
  • Use clear, simple language
  • Number the steps
  • Include cooking temperatures and times
