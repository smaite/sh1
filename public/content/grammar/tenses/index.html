Master past, present, and future tenses

Understanding Tenses

Present Tenses

  • Simple Present: Habits and facts (I play football)
  • Present Continuous: Actions happening now (I am playing)
  • Present Perfect: Completed actions with present results (I have played)
  • Present Perfect Continuous: Actions continuing until now (I have been playing)

Past Tenses

  • Simple Past: Completed actions in the past (I played)
  • Past Continuous: Ongoing actions in the past (I was playing)
  • Past Perfect: Actions before another past action (I had played)
  • Past Perfect Continuous: Duration before another past action (I had been playing)

Future Tenses

  • Simple Future: Predictions and promises (I will play)
  • Future Continuous: Ongoing future actions (I will be playing)
  • Future Perfect: Completion before a future time (I will have played)
  • Future Perfect Continuous: Duration up to a future time (I will have been playing)
